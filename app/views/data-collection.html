Data collection


Data about learners is collected from a range of training providers and employers, to evidence training delivered. For the majority, this data is the Individualised Learner Record (ILR) data and is returned monthly. The data is validated using current reference data. The verified data feeds into the Funding and Contracting Service for payment and to Data Science for reporting.
