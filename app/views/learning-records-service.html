Learning record service (LRS)


The service offers a suite of IT products and supports the principle to share information with users across the education sector. The Unique Learner Number (ULN) is a 10-digit identifier assigned learners aged 14 and above. Linked to the ULN is the Personal Learning Record (PLR). The PLR is an on-line record which is made up of participation and achievement data. The service also offers a Learner Plan. The Learner Plan is used by prisons/offender training providers to record progression of offenders even if they move locations.
